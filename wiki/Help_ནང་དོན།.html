Welcome!

Hello, ནང་དོན།, and welcome to Dzongkha Wikipedia! We hope you like the place and decide to stay. Here are a few good links for newcomers:

  • Talk about Dzongkha Wikipedia
  • The five pillars of Wikipedia
  • How to edit a page
  • Help pages
  • Tutorial
  • How to write a great article
  • Manual of Style

I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian! Please sign your name on talk pages using four tildes (~~~~); this will automatically produce your name and the date. If you need help, check out en:Wikipedia:Where to ask a question, ask me on my talk page, or place {{helpme}} on your talk page and someone will show up shortly to answer your questions. Again, welcome!
