Technologies[edit]

These are technologies related to the development, testing, deployment, data storage, etc.. in Android applications we should look at.

Deployment[edit]

Google Play Store[edit]

The primary deployment option I think we should look at first due to its wide acceptance, there are other options but for now, let's focus on this.

Player Experience[edit]

Unity Graphics[edit]

High learning curve but very high-end results, if we can figure it out. No in-house experience with this...

HTML Graphics[edit]

We have in-house experience and depending on the need it will most likely be the graphics interface we will initially use.

Income Generation[edit]

We need to start educating ourselves on how to integrate advertising into the application. The odds are we aren't going to make money at first, but who knows we may get lucky... :-)

Google Play[edit]
