Once a month, I get to play bass in the church "backup band" (aka "Team Two").

This is my repository of music for printing out when I play.

All songs are in a variation of the ChordPro markup, but are processed by a JavaScript library I wrote to make them more readily printed.

A good link for lyrics is http://CyberHymnal.org
